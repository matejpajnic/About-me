Hiking

In my opinion, hiking and backpacking is not a sport. If you are comparing your pace or endurance against others then I personally feel that you are missing the point.

For me, walking gives me the opportunity to discover things in the world that fascinate me while at the same time I am nurturing my mental and physical health. I love walking with people who enjoy good conversation, and a bit of adventure. I enjoy taking a few moments to take videos and photos of animals and flora.

A good hike should be enjoyed and not rushed. If you take your time to absorb your surroundings you will end up creating a library of beautiful memories and a sense of peace that seeps naturally into your daily life.
